My Vidhik

"Vidhik", developed by Peraturb Decimo LLP,is an innovative digital platform crafted to empower advocates practicing in Indian courts. As a registered Limited Liability Partnership with the ROC Gwalior, Madhya Pradesh, India,, and an MSME-registered startup recognized by the Government of India, Vidhik is at the forefront of transforming legal practice.

Key Features of Vidhik:
  • Activity Feed: A unique space for advocates to share their knowledge, insights, and experiences with peers.
  • Extensive Digital Library: Access to over 1.35 million judgments, ranging from the Privy Council to the present day.
  • Digital Case Diary: Includes sub-diaries and preformatted applications to simplify case management.
  • Secure Chat Rooms: Facilitate collaborative problem-solving among advocates.
  • Nationwide Advocate Directory: Enables the public to easily find and connect with legal professionals.
  • Seamless Conference Scheduling: Simplifies the process of arranging meetings between advocates and clients.
Additionally, Vidhik features:
  • Time Pass: A dedicated gaming zone for relaxation and entertainment.
  • Market Place: A shopping platform offering a variety of merchandise.
By streamlining legal workflows and enhancing connectivity, Vidhik is revolutionizing the landscape of the Indian legal profession, making it more efficient and accessible for everyone involved.
